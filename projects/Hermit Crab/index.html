Hermit Crab

As my final individual project for CSCI 1280: Intermediate 3D Computer Animation, I used Maya and Unity to create a playable character and traversable environment. For a visual explanation of the production pipeline, continue watching the video above.

3D Art

I used Maya to model low poly environment assets and the hermit crab playable character. I rigged the character then exported everything to Unity.

Programming

In Unity, I used the Universal Render Pipeline, the shader graph, and HLSL to create a custom toon shader for my scene. I also coded the player movement, camera movement, and animations.

Composing

I used MuseScore to compose an original loopable track for the game that sought to enhance the beachy vibes of the experience.
